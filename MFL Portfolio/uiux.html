BIG BIRD GAME

The project objective is to create a mobile app that encourages users to become more involved in the recreational facilities Wellington City Council has to offer. The goal is to create a holistic and immersive experience that combines digital entertainment with Wellingtons unique recreational facilities.

BRIEF

Team Project

Team Project

PROJECT DIRECTION

The problem regarding this project brief, is the lack of young individuals getting involved in Wellingtons recreational facilities. The solution to this would be to create an effective and engaging SmartPhone App, designed to encourage young individuals to get more involved with the local recreational facilities Wellington has to offer. To achieve this, we will conduct various research methods to better investigate our target audience in order to produce a design solution for the Wellington City Council.

PRODUCT

PROTOTYPE

The developed prototype represents a synthesis of feedback from the hifi wireframes and aims to strike a balance between minimalism and engagement. The inclusion of consistent directional cues and strategic use of colours and opacity enhances the usability and legibility of the design. By simplifying the overall design and providing an intuitive user experience, the design aligns with the needs and preferences of young teenage users, creating a playful and comprehensive app design that fosters engagement and enjoyment.

Overview

The Big bird App was designed and presented through Figma, with functioning buttons, animations, transitions, user input and more. Through Photoshop, After Effects, and Illustrator, we were able to create an immersive and engaging app prototype, to reach our project goals.
